Survey Information

Target Audience

People who travel a short distance (16km or lesser) for their commute

Methodology

Random | (This form was given randomly to people to fill out)

Timeline

31 January 2022 to 11 February 2022

List of survey questions:

How far do you travel for your daily commute?

How often do you cycle?

How likely are you going to cycle for your daily commute?

Which of the following is the most likely reason you would be discouraged to cycle?

How do you think the cycling experience can be elevated to encourage more people to cycle or ride a PMD to commute

Do you think providing infrastructure for people to rest in and have shelter would encourage more people to cycle?

Would you be more encouraged to commute via cycling/PMD if there were rest stops?

What are your opinions on the current cycling paths?

Do you think more people would commute if they could exchange credits they collect when they cycle for vouchers?

Any other comments?
