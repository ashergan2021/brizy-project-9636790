Leveraging Technology

Leveraging technology refers to the use of technology to make significant changes in an industry. By using technology to create increasing value, with existing and/or scarce resources, it will allow the industry to evolve at a faster rate with improved user experiences.

Car-Lite Modes of Transport

In our urban living environment, going car-lite encourages the move towards a liveable and sustainable environment, but also gives streets back to people.


In a ideal situation there will be less use of private transport and more use of public transport, walking and cycling.

Leveraging Technology to Promote Car-lite Modes of Transport

Key Highlights

Sustainable transport is a key pillar of the Singapore Green Plan 2030, with "walk, cycle, ride" as the preferred way to get around.

The plan is to have 90 per cent of all peak period journeys made by "walk, cycle, ride" modes by 2040.

Challenges we face on Public Transport

Several short and long delays may happen time to time, some caused by the timings of the train or bus ahead, while some are malfunction of the system

Cycling infrastructure is not as well- developed hence many people do not prefer cycling over other modes of transportation(e.g. Small paths and not clearly marked)

PMD and Cycling in Singapore’s hot weather discourages people from using it as a form of transport.

PMD and Cycling in Singapore’s hot weather discourages people from using it as a form of transport was chosen as Problem Statement

Chosen Research Title

Building infrastructure to help cyclist to deal with bad weather and exhaustion is the most plausible strategy to promote car-lite modes of transport (Walk Cycle Ride) in Singapore.
